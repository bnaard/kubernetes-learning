% === Pod Disruption Budgets ================================================
\tcbsubtitle{Overview}
{\fontsize{6.5}{7.5}\selectfont\sffamily
A PDB limits how many Pods of a workload can be \textbf{voluntarily disrupted} at the same time (e.g.\ during \texttt{k drain}, cluster upgrades, autoscaler scale-down). Does \emph{not} protect against involuntary disruptions (node crash, OOM kill).\par}

\tcbsubtitle{Imperative}
\begin{cheatcodebash}
k get pdb -A
k describe pdb <name>
\end{cheatcodebash}

\tcbsubtitle{Spec Essentials}
\cmdhead{Using minAvailable}
\begin{cheatcodebashbold}
apiVersion: policy/v1
kind: PodDisruptionBudget
metadata: {name: web-pdb}
spec:
  minAvailable: 2      # or "80%" — at least this many must stay
  selector:
    matchLabels: {app: web} # must match the Pods to protect
\end{cheatcodebashbold}

\cmdhead{Using maxUnavailable}
\begin{cheatcodebash}
spec:
  maxUnavailable: 1    # or "20%" — at most this many can be down
  selector:
    matchLabels: {app: web}
\end{cheatcodebash}

\note{Set \textbf{either} \texttt{minAvailable} or \texttt{maxUnavailable} — not both.}

  Field & Meaning \\
  \texttt{minAvailable} & Min Pods that must remain running (absolute or \%) \\
  \texttt{maxUnavailable} & Max Pods that can be disrupted (absolute or \%) \\
  \texttt{selector} & Label selector matching the target Pods \\
  \texttt{status.disruptionsAllowed} & Current number of Pods that can be evicted \\
  \texttt{status.currentHealthy} & Number of healthy Pods right now \\

\end{tcolorbox}

\tcbsubtitle{Voluntary vs Involuntary Disruptions}
\begin{tcolorbox}
    [
    skin=cheatboxtablesimple,
    before skip=0pt,
    after skip=0pt,
    tabularray={
    colspec={X[1,l]X[1,l]},
    columns={valign=t, colsep=0.4em},
    rows={rowsep=0pt, font=\sffamily\fontsize{6}{7}\selectfont},
    hline{2}={solid, wd=0.3pt, fg=gray!40},
    vline{2-3}={dash=solid, wd=0.3pt, fg=gray!30},
    row{1}={font=\sffamily\fontsize{6}{7}\selectfont\bfseries},
    }
    ]
  Voluntary (PDB protects) & Involuntary (PDB does not) \\
  \texttt{k drain <node>} & Node hardware failure \\
  Cluster autoscaler scale-down & OOM kill \\
  \texttt{k delete pod} (via Eviction API) & Kernel panic \\
  Rolling upgrades & Preemption by higher-priority Pod \\

\end{tcolorbox}
